## рҹ“– About

<p align="center">
  <em>"This is not a course. This is a simulation of the most demanding trading desk in the region."</em>
</p>

NovaCap drops you into the role of a **quantitative trading strategist** aboard a proprietary trading desk. Every trade, every risk report, every backtest iteration runs through the same pipeline an actual quant firm would use:

<p align="center">
  <code>Market Data  вҶ’  Signal  вҶ’  Execution  вҶ’  Ledger  вҶ’  Risk  вҶ’  Evaluation</code>
</p>

The only difference? The market is synthetic and the capital is fake. **The lessons are not.**

Built on six integrated agent layers, NovaCap is a self-contained ecosystem with HMM regime-switching dynamics, price-time priority limit order books, SQLite double-entry accounting, VaR/CVaR risk guards, and AST-level code auditing вҖ” running on the **Python standard library alone**.

## вңЁ Features

### рҹ“Ҡ Market Engine
- вң… **Two-regime HMM** вҖ” low-vol trending (Пғ=0.003), high-vol mean-reverting (Пғ=0.025)
- вң… **Jump diffusion** вҖ” Poisson-distributed shocks with configurable intensity
- вң… **Regime-dependent depth** вҖ” 12-level book in trending, 6-level in volatile
- вң… **7 correlated symbols** вҖ” BTC/USD, ETH/USDT, SOL/USD, EUR/USD, and more
- вң… **Depth-based slippage** вҖ” walks book levels for VWAP estimation

### вҡЎ Execution
- вң… **Full limit order book** вҖ” price-time priority queues
- вң… **4 order types** вҖ” Market, Limit (GTC), IOC, FOK
- вң… **Partial fills** вҖ” queue-based matching with remainder logic
- вң… **Order management** вҖ” track, cancel, and query live orders

### рҹ“’ Accounting
- вң… **SQLite double-entry** вҖ” 9 standard accounts (Cash, Inventory, Fees, P&L...)
- вң… **Per-symbol P&L** вҖ” trade-level profit/loss with fee recording
- вң… **Integrity verification** вҖ” automatic debits = credits check
- вң… **Audit trail** вҖ” full trade history with timestamps

### рҹӣЎпёҸ Risk Management
- вң… **Value at Risk (VaR)** вҖ” historical 95% confidence
- вң… **Conditional VaR (CVaR)** вҖ” expected shortfall beyond VaR
- вң… **Circuit breaker** вҖ” 5% drawdown over 10-bar window
- вң… **Position limits** вҖ” 15% of capital per symbol
- вң… **Leverage cap** вҖ” 2.0Г— gross exposure maximum
- вң… **Portfolio correlation** вҖ” real-time inter-symbol tracking

### рҹ”¬ Backtesting
- вң… **Monte Carlo engine** вҖ” N iterations Г— D days in subprocess isolation
- вң… **Distribution statistics** вҖ” mean, median, P10, P90 final equity
- вң… **Sharpe analysis** вҖ” probability of risk-adjusted return > 1.0
- вң… **Max drawdown distribution** вҖ” severity range across all runs

### рҹ”Қ Evaluation
- вң… **AST static auditor** вҖ” 9 check categories (eval, loops, annotations, globals...)
- вң… **Hidden test suite** вҖ” isolated subprocess, 30s timeout
- вң… **Composite scoring** вҖ” 0вҖ“100 with weighted deductions
- вң… **90-day curriculum** вҖ” 3 phases across 3 market regimes

### рҹҸ—пёҸ Infrastructure
- вң… **Concurrent HTTP** вҖ” mock exchange on `:8080`, web UI on `:8081`
- вң… **Persistent state** вҖ” `save_state.json` with crash recovery
- вң… **CI/CD** вҖ” GitHub Actions: 19 stages Г— 5 Python versions (3.8вҖ“3.12)
- вң… **Containerized** вҖ” Dockerfile with `python:3.11-slim`

## рҹҢӘпёҸ Regime-Switching Market Engine

The `MockPriceGenerator` implements a two-regime Markov-switching model:

| Regime | Пғ | Mean Reversion | Trend Bias | Spread | Jump О» | Book Depth |
|--------|----|----------------|------------|--------|--------|------------|
| 0 вҖ” LOW_VOL_TRENDING | `0.003` | `0.002` | `+0.0004` | `0.8Г—` | `0.002` | 12 levels |
| 1 вҖ” HIGH_VOL_MEAN_REVERTING | `0.025` | `0.08` | `вҲ’0.0001` | `1.8Г—` | `0.015` | 6 levels |

**Transition matrix:**
- `P(0 вҶ’ 1) = 0.15` вҖ” minimum 30 ticks in regime 0
- `P(1 вҶ’ 0) = 0.30` вҖ” minimum 20 ticks in regime 1

Regime state is exposed via `GET /v1/regime` and embedded in every order book and ticker response, allowing strategies to adapt to changing market conditions.

## рҹ“Ӣ Order Types & Execution

| Type | Behavior |
|------|----------|
| **Market** | Executes immediately against limit book. Unfilled portion fills at mid-price with slippage. |
| **Limit (GTC)** | Placed in price-time priority queue. Remains until filled or cancelled. |
| **IOC** | Immediate-or-Cancel вҖ” fills against available book, cancels remainder. |
| **FOK** | Fill-or-Kill вҖ” entire quantity must fill or order is cancelled. |

**Slippage model:**
```python
slippage_bps = pg.estimate_slippage_bps("BTC/USD", 1.0, "buy")
```

The model walks book levels until quantity is filled, computing volume-weighted average price vs. mid-price. Orders exceeding full depth incur a 5% penalty.

| Fee | Value |
|-----|-------|
| Exchange fee | 10 bps (0.1%) per trade |
| Recording | Debit `4100 Fee Expense`, Credit `1000 Cash - USD` |
